Lasagna

Our Lasagna is truly special. We use fresh made ingredients straight from our garden. You would'nt believe the amazing flavors and textures.

Don't worry, we're not going to leave your mouth watering. We have made an easy to use recipe so you can make this Lasagna at home! Make sure to read the instructions carefully and you'll be golden.

  • Tomatoes
  • Tomatoe Paste
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Seasoning
  • Onion

Step By Step

  1. Mash all the tomatoes into a bowl.
  2. Cut all of your vegetables and saute
  3. Season tomatoe sauce in the bowl
  4. Add tomatoe paste to sauteed vegies
  5. Add your tomatoe sauce into the pot
  6. Let simmer for atleast two hours
  7. Stir occasionally
